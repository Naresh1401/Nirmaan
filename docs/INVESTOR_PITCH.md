## Problem

India's construction industry is a **$800B market** growing at 8% annually. Yet material procurement — worth **$150B+** — operates like it's 1990:

- **Fragmented Supply**: 10M+ unorganized suppliers with no digital presence
- **Price Opacity**: Same cement bag costs ₹380–₹450 within 5 km radius
- **Logistics Chaos**: No reliable delivery — 40% of orders face delays
- **Zero Discovery**: Builders rely on word-of-mouth for new suppliers
- **Manual Procurement**: Contractors manage ₹1Cr+ projects on paper/WhatsApp

**Result**: Builders waste 15–20% of their budget on inefficiency.
