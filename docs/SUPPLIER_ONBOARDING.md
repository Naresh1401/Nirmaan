## Onboarding Process

### Step 1: Identification & Outreach (Week 1–2)

**Field Agent Activities:**
- Map all construction material suppliers in the city
- Categorize by material type, size, and location
- Prioritize top 20 suppliers by volume and reputation
- In-person visits with product demo on tablet

**Pitch Points:**
- "Get orders from customers you've never reached"
- "Zero investment — we bring the customers to you"
- "Your shop stays open 24/7 online"
- "Free for the first 3 months"

### Step 2: Registration & Verification (Day 1–3)

**Documents Collected:**
- GST registration certificate
- Business PAN card
- Shop/warehouse photos (min 5)
- Owner Aadhaar/ID proof
- Bank account details (for payments)
- Trade license (if applicable)

**Verification Process:**
1. Field agent photographs shop, stock, and documents
2. Phone verification of owner
3. GST validation via government API
4. Bank account verification via penny drop
5. Approval within 24 hours

### Step 3: Catalog Setup (Day 2–5)

**Field Agent Assists With:**
- Photographing all products
- Entering product names in local language + English
- Setting prices (MRP and selling price)
- Defining stock quantities
- Setting delivery radius
- Configuring minimum order values

**Product Catalog Template:**
```
Material: UltraTech Cement OPC 53 Grade
Category: Cement
Unit: 50 kg bag
MRP: ₹420
Selling Price: ₹395
Stock: 500 bags
Min Order: 10 bags
Delivery Radius: 25 km
```

### Step 4: Training (Day 3–7)

**Supplier Training Modules:**
1. **App Walkthrough** (30 min) — How to manage orders, update stock
2. **Order Management** (20 min) — Accept/reject orders, partial fulfillment
3. **Pricing Strategy** (15 min) — Competitive pricing, bulk discounts
4. **Payment & Settlement** (15 min) — How and when they get paid
5. **Quality Guidelines** (15 min) — What customers expect

**Training Delivery:**
- In-person at supplier location
- Video tutorials in Telugu/Hindi
- WhatsApp support group for suppliers

### Step 5: Go-Live Support (Week 1–2)

- Field agent present for first 5 orders
- Handholding through order acceptance and fulfillment
- Immediate issue resolution
- Daily check-in calls for first 2 weeks

## Building Supplier Trust

### Challenge: "Why should I trust a digital platform?"

**Trust-Building Mechanisms:**

1. **Guaranteed Payments**
   - Payment settled within T+2 (2 business days)
   - Daily settlement option for Gold suppliers
   - Clear payment dashboard with transaction history

2. **No Stock Risk**
   - Supplier only fulfills confirmed orders
   - No inventory commitment required
   - Can update stock in real-time

3. **Transparent Process**
   - Supplier sees customer details before accepting
   - Clear commission structure (no hidden fees)
   - Order tracking visible to supplier

4. **Local Presence**
   - Field agents are from the same city
   - Office/presence in local market area
   - Attend local business association meetings

5. **Success Stories**
   - Showcase early adopter success metrics
   - Video testimonials from successful suppliers
   - Monthly "Top Supplier" recognition

6. **Gradual Onboarding**
   - Start with existing customers placing orders through app
   - Show incremental new customer orders over time
   - Build confidence before expanding
