# Growth Strategy — Nirmaan

## Three-Phase Expansion Plan

---

## Phase 1: City Launch — Peddapalli (Months 1–6)

### Goals
- Onboard **50+ suppliers** across all material categories
- Onboard **100+ delivery partners** (truck/tractor/auto operators)
- Acquire **1,000+ registered users**
- Process **40+ orders/day** by month 6

### Execution Plan

**Month 1–2: Foundation**
- Deploy platform MVP
- Hire 3 field agents for supplier onboarding
- Partner with 10 hardware stores as anchor suppliers
- Set up warehouse/staging area for logistics coordination
- Launch with cement, sand, bricks, steel (core categories)

**Month 3–4: Growth**
- Expand to tiles, paint, plumbing, electrical materials
- Launch contractor referral program
- Begin construction-site marketing
- Enable bulk order capabilities
- Implement delivery tracking

**Month 5–6: Optimization**
- Achieve product-market fit signals (repeat order rate > 30%)
- Optimize delivery routes and costs
- Launch supplier subscription plans
- Build case studies from early customers
- Prepare regional expansion playbook

## Customer Acquisition Strategy

### Offline Channels (Primary for Phase 1–2)

1. **Construction Site Marketing**
   - Field agents visit active construction sites
   - Demonstrate app, help place first order
   - Offer ₹500 discount on first order

2. **Hardware Shop Partnerships**
   - Co-branded signage at partner shops
   - Shop owners earn referral commission (₹100/new customer)
   - Joint promotions

3. **Contractor Partnerships**
   - Identify top 50 contractors in each city
   - Offer dedicated account management
   - Bulk pricing advantages
   - Monthly credit facility

4. **Builder Associations**
   - Present at local builder association meetings
   - Sponsor construction industry events
   - Partner with architecture firms

### Digital Channels (Scale in Phase 2–3)

1. **Google Ads** — Target "buy cement online", "sand delivery near me"
2. **YouTube** — Construction tips, material guides (Telugu/Hindi)
3. **WhatsApp Marketing** — Order updates, offers, new product alerts
4. **Facebook/Instagram** — Before/after construction photos, testimonials
5. **SEO** — Local SEO for each city ("building materials in Peddapalli")

### Referral Program

| Action | Reward |
|--------|--------|
| Refer a Customer | ₹200 credit (both parties) |
| Refer a Supplier | ₹1,000 credit |
| Refer a Delivery Partner | ₹500 credit |
| 5+ Referrals in a Month | Gold status + extra ₹500 |

## Retention Strategy

1. **Loyalty Program**: Points per order, redeemable for discounts
2. **Repeat Order Discounts**: 2% extra discount on 5th+ order
3. **Project Dashboard**: Track all procurement for a construction project
4. **Price Alerts**: Notify when materials drop in price
5. **Scheduled Orders**: Auto-reorder recurring materials
