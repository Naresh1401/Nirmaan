Update #3 – Nirmaan Is Taking Shape 🏗️

A few weeks ago, I shared the idea behind Nirmaan — a platform to simplify construction material sourcing and project coordination in India.

Since then, the response has been incredible. Thank you to everyone who reached out with feedback, ideas and encouragement. Every conversation has helped shape the direction of what we are building.

Today, I want to share what Nirmaan actually looks like now.

🎥 Here is a short walkthrough of the platform we have been building.

What we have built so far

Nirmaan is no longer just an idea. It is a working platform with real features designed to solve real problems for builders, contractors and homeowners.

Here is what is live today:

🛒 Materials Marketplace
A searchable catalog of 50,000+ construction products across categories — cement, steel, bricks, sand, aggregates, tiles, paints, electrical, plumbing and more. Builders can browse, compare and order materials from verified suppliers in one place.

🤖 SETU – AI Civil Engineering Consultant
This is something I am personally excited about. SETU is our AI-powered construction intelligence system. It can:
• Design beams, columns, slabs and footings using IS 456 calculations
• Recommend the right cement, steel grade or brick type for your project
• Estimate construction costs per sqft with stage-wise breakdowns
• Diagnose cracks, waterproofing issues and concrete defects
• Plan project timelines with milestone tracking
• Answer technical questions with references to Indian Standard codes

SETU is not a generic chatbot. It is built specifically for Indian construction — with knowledge of IS codes, NBC 2016, Telangana rates and practical site experience.

📐 AI Cost Estimator
Enter your plot size and building type, and the estimator generates a detailed material quantity breakdown with cost estimates — for houses, roads, bridges, compound walls, water tanks and more.

📦 Delivery & Logistics
Materials delivered directly to your construction site with order tracking and scheduled delivery.

💳 Construction Credit
Buy now, pay later facility for material purchases with flexible repayment options.

👷 Workforce Connect
Find verified masons, electricians, plumbers, carpenters and other skilled workers for your project.

🔧 Equipment Rental
Rent JCBs, concrete mixers, cranes, scaffolding and other construction equipment at competitive rates.

🎨 Design Studio
Browse pre-designed floor plans and customize them for your plot — with Vastu-compliant options available.

🌐 Digital Twin
3D visualization of your construction project for better planning and progress tracking.

👑 Nirmaan Premium
Unlimited AI consultations, priority delivery, exclusive material discounts, dedicated support and advanced project tools.

🔐 Admin Dashboard
Complete backend management system for orders, payments, inventory, suppliers, deliveries, analytics and user management.

The bigger picture

What started as a solution for material procurement has grown into something larger.

Nirmaan is becoming a construction intelligence platform — combining a materials marketplace, AI-powered engineering support, project coordination tools and a network of verified suppliers and professionals.

The goal remains the same: make construction more organized, transparent and efficient for everyone involved.

What is next

We are continuing to build and improve. Some things on the roadmap:
• Expanding supplier network across more cities and districts
• Strengthening the AI with project memory and personalized recommendations
• Adding more estimation tools and design templates
• Building mobile app for on-site access
• Onboarding more suppliers, contractors and service providers
